About Us

Pramila Electricals is a trusted name in the electrical equipment industry, specializing in high-quality 11KV electrical components and solutions. Based in Sant Ravidas Nagar (Bhadohi), we have been serving industrial clients across India with reliable, safety-certified electrical products.

Our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and timely delivery has made us a preferred choice for electrical contractors, industries, and infrastructure projects. We source our products from reputed manufacturers ensuring durability and compliance with industry standards.
